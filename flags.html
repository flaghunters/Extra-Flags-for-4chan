About flags on 4chan

The updated world template can be found here: https://gitlab.com/flagtism/Extra-Flags-for-4chan/-/raw/master/maps/World.png

More can be discussed at the /flag/ general on /int/.

By default, the flags on posts are cut out from the flagsheet shown on the left. It can be found here:
https://s.4cdn.org/image/flags.8.png

There are 269 flags in total, 259 of which are included in this flag sheet. The ten absent flags (A1, A2, AP, AQ, LY, ME, MM, VA, UZ, and XK) are elaborated on below.

 
Several 'stealth' flags exist; these are identical to others:
France (FR)
French Guiana (GF)
Réunion (RE)
Saint Barthélemy (BL)
Bonaire, Sint Eustatius and Saba (BQ)
Netherlands (NL)
Bouvet Island (BV)
Norway (NO)
Svalbard and Jan Mayen (SJ)
Australia (AU)
Heard Island and the MacDonald Islands (HM)
The boxes between Iceland and Greenland are for flags present on the 4chan server, or were once on the server but got changed. These flags are displayed in boxes as they do not correspond to any territory as shown on the map, as they either are non-territorial entities or will cause overlaps if attempted to be displayed on the map.

A1 and A2 are anonymous proxy and satellite provider respectively, and show up as Andorran flags that are labelled 'Unknown' when hovered over. These direct to a.gif when clicked on.
XX is a blank white flag with a question mark on it. It occurs most often when a thread is moved from another board. It shows up as 'Unknown' when hovered over and directs to xx.gif when clicked on.
AP and EU are the Asia-Pacific region and Europe respectively. AP shows up as an Andorran flag that is labelled 'Unknown' when hovered over, but directs to ap.gif when clicked on. EU shows up as the European flag.
AQ represents the old flag of Antarctica. LY is the flag of the Libyan Arab Jamahiriya, which is no longer in use. ME is an old version of the flag of Montenegro, it is 16x12 instead of 16x11. MM is the old flag of Myanmar. UZ is the old flag of Uzbekistan, the colour differs from the current one. VA is the old rectangular version of the flag of the Holy See. XK represents Kosovo before the flag got properly added to the flagsheet. All of them have been changed on the 4chan server.
AN and CS are the Netherlands Antilles and Serbia and Montenegro respectively. Both are entities that no longer exist but remain flags on the 4chan server.
GB and ES represent the four flags of England, Scotland, Wales, and Catalonia. These are included on the template as they are flags on the 4chan server.
FAM is the flag of the company that made the default flag icons; its flag is likewise on the server.

*It should be noted that unlike the three pseudo-Andorran flags shown above (A1, A2, and AP), actual posts from Andorra will be labelled 'Andorra' when hovered over and direct to ad.gif when clicked on.

*The flags of England, Scotland, Wales, and Catalonia on the 4chan server are not identiccal to those used in Extra Flags.

The flags labelled with blue or red text in the boxes above, as well as Bouvet Island (BV), Heard Island and the MacDonald Islands (HM), and Western Sahara (EH) are unobtainable legitimately. They do not have Internet service providers, but it is possible to post with these flags using a proxy. All three of them have already been posted with on 4chan.
